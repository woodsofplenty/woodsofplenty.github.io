Our Local Friends

Elderberry

A tall fan shaped plant that grows in most soil types and tolerates any amount of sunlight. Produces great berries and flowers for making wine.


Blackberry

A sturdy, sun-loving cane with thorns. Produces large, juicy, and flavorful berries.


American Plum

Spreads wide in open fields. Great for windbreaks and highway or riverside plantings. Produces a delicious fruit.


American Persimmon

Grows in open sun or under the canopy. Fruit hangs on the tree and ripens in late fall.


American Hazelnut

This well shaped suckering shrub supports wildlife and produces prolifically.


Pawpaw

North America's largest native fruit. Has a tropical flavor similar to bananna and mango custard.
