Develop a new connection

Woods of Plenty charges for labor by the hour. Our labor rates in 2015 are $65/hr for Gavin, the forest designer, and $65/hr for Jeff the implementation engineer. Woods of Plenty often works with a skilled team of specialists, driving the cost per acre down and the impact per time up. Talk to us - we can work with your budget.


Forest Constulation

Bring us out to your land to discuss your goals, help us to better understand the situation, and learn more about the forest. The situation is different in every forest, and we rely on consultations to generate an estimate of the time required to achieve goals.
Our forest consultation fee is $440 + travel costs. If you hire Woods of Plenty, half of this money will be put towards future labor.
