Develop a new connection

Our work flow begins with a consultation, through which we learn the about the situation and initiate land preparation. This involves invasive plant management and dynamic design. We then perform a custom planting to being the growth of future food.

There are many ways to frame our work. Read on for consultation info and project ideas. Talk to us - we can work with your budget.


Forest Constulation

Bring us out to your land to discuss your goals. All land is unique and we rely on consultations to gather information to generate projects tailored to your needs. After a consultation, we provide a consultation document complete with our insights, proposed projects, and illustrated site maps.

Fee: $440 + travel costs.
If you hire Woods of Plenty, half of this money will be put towards future labor.

Non-traditional Orchard

Hardy food-producing native plants with no operational costs.


Niche food propagation

Perennial native fruiting plants in the forest are the future of food! There are spots in your forest perfect for planting them.

Soft edge (ecotone)

Woods of Plenty brings the magic back to hard-edged forests for abundant food production!

Old Orchard Revival

Have an old orchard being dragged down by invasive plants? Chances are, it's still producing nice fruit!
Best part is, the lack of management over the years means non-toxic food.

Naturalized Areas and Rain Gardens

Minimize future work and create form through plant propagation and the utilization of other resources on site. These installations capture life's most precious resource and provide habitat for pollinators.

Perennial Kitchen Gardens

Around the house there are opportunities to incorporate perennial garden crops into existing garden beds and to establish new beds for planting.

Wildlife habitats

Let us know what you want to attract and we can develop your forest to suit their needs.

Campsite & Trails

Be guided to places of interest in your forest. We can design and establish a comfortable camp site for you and your crew to enjoy life outdoors.
Features include: Seating, native grass bedding, firepit, windbreak trellises, nooks for tents, sawdust toilet, and fruiting trees and plants.

Bittersweet and Grape Vine Cut

A great starting point to drastically improve tree health. Gavin and Jeff can cut 1 acre dense with vines in 2 hours. They make about 1200 vine cuts per hour (they've counted).
