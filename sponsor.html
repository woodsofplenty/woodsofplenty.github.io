Become a Forest Sponsor


Sponsor our work on public lands

We love improving the forest and want to share Woods of Plenty™ with everyone! Over the years we have spent countless hours working in public parks. With your sponsorship we can continue to devote our time to improving public land.

When you click the sponsor button you will be taken to paypal. There you can enter any amount for your sponsorship. Afterwards you will be returned to this site. With every dollar of your support Woods of Plenty™ will conduct positive forest stewardship on your behalf.



We will send you before and after photos of the site worked plus a personal note about our experience working in the area.

This is not a donation. Thus, it is not tax deductable. Woods of Plenty™ is a for profit business with a passion for serving the public and the environment.
