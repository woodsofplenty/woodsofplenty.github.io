We want to improve your forest

We tackle dense and tangled forest

Prior land usage created seemingly impenetrable forests. Invasive plant removal through human power can revitalize stagnant forest land. Future management occurs now through our efforts.

A truly light operation

We move on foot and carry our own tools. We do not use heavy machinery and do not spray chemicals. Our methods preserve pre-existing native plant material assets.

We introduce native fruiting and flowering plants

Added diversity creates stablilty, eventually leading to a self-maintaining forest. Native plants will thrive in balance without intervention. Healthy forests provide for humans, animals, and themselves.

A lasting legacy

We transform unusable land into valuable forest, creating an accessible forest worth experiencing. Share nature with all you love!
