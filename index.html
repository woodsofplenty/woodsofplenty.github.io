Nature-Grown Food



Welcome. Here you will encounter fresh fruit and nuts at every turn, growing reliably year after year to feed you and your family. In time this source of incredible food will produce more than you can harvest; enjoy the best nature can provide.

This place may sound like a fable, but it can be the reality of your land. This place is not a farm or a garden. Rather, it is a Woods of Plenty.

Led by a designer and an engineer Woods of Plenty helps landowners establish their property as a resilient long-term source of non-toxic food. With the intimate knowledge gained from studying and observing the forest, our team plants native fruit and nut producing species that increase biodiversity and food production.

Tackle dense tangled overgrowth

Introduced species have created seemingly impenetrable thickets. Invasive plant management through human power will revitalize stagnant land. Through our efforts, future growth will occur as you desire.

A truly light operation

We move on foot and carry our own tools. We do not use heavy machinery and do not use chemicals. Our methods support advanced regeneration by preserving pre-existing native plant assets and essential organisms in the soil.

Food at your fingertips

Added diversity creates stabilty, eventually leading to a self-maintaining ecosystem. Native fruiting and flowering plants will thrive in balance without intervention. Woods of Plenty provide for humans, animals, and themselves.

A lasting legacy

Through innovative invasives management and bioengineering we transform unusable land into valuable, food-producing Woods of Plenty. We create as we work and deliver access to land worth experiencing. Secure your family's food supply for generations!
