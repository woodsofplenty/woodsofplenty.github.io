Bringing Woods of Plenty Home

Add value and curb-appeal to your landscape with custom plant bundles!

Young plants help keep our installation costs low, are more vigorous growers, and establish an early connection with the soil rendering them more disease-resistant, drought-tolerant, and winter-hardy.

We can also remove existing drab shrubbery and chip it on site, leaving mulch for the new planting. A project typically takes one day to complete.
